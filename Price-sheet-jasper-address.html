Entering a Price Sheet Jasper Address


 

Select the Address bounce bar menu option.

On the Price Sheet Remit To pop-up screen, enter or edit the Address, City, State, Zip, Phone and Fax entries in their respective fields. Entering the final digit or pressing the enter key from the Fax field will save and close your changes.
