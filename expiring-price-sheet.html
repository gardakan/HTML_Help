Expiring a Price Sheet


 

Expiring a Price Sheet automatically changes the expiration date to the current date.

Make sure that the price sheet you wish to expire is displayed, and select the Expire bounce bar menu option.

Select "Yes" to confirm the expiration. Selecting "No" or pressing the escape key will cancel the expiration process and return to the previous screen.

A pop-up message will display to confirm that the sheet has now been expired. Press enter to complete the process.
