Terminology


 




Term Description

Standard price sheet

 

Price sheet to which to attach item pricing for a customer

 

Promotional price sheet

 

Promotional price sheets are attached to an existing price sheet, and contain special/limited item pricing.

 

Draft

 

When adding a price sheet, it will initially be a draft.

 

Proposal

 

A draft can be changed to a Proposal via the 'Change' option on the price sheet detail screen bounce bar.

 

Price Sheet

 

A proposal needs to have items attached to it and be approved by an authorized user in order to become a price sheet.

 

Full Load Weight

 

Total order weight of a full truck load.

 

Order Quantity:

Full Pallets

 

Under order quantity, a full pallet represents a fully-loaded pallet containing only items from the order.

 

Order Quantity:

Full Layers

 

Under order quantity, a full layer represents a complete pallet layer containing only items from the order.

 

Order Quantity:

As Desired

 

Under order quantity, represents any order quantity other than a full layer or a full pallet.

 

Minimum Shipment (ULV)

 

The minimum order quantity in ULV (Unit Load Volume).  This volume is the amount as defined in the "Order Quantity" section.

 

Prepaid minimum unit load value

 

Minimum ULV order quantity on prepaid orders

 

Lead Time

 

Number of currently elapsed business days for the price sheet order range. This offsets the order range from the ship range by the number of lead time business (Monday-Friday) days.

 

R/S/S

 

Reclamation / Salvage / Spoils

 

P/U Allowance

 

Level 1 - 6

 

Levels 1 through 6 allow for setting different pricing levels based on order quantity.
